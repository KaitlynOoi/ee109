2. (1 point) Briefly explain why updating the LCD display in your ISR could
lead to undesirable behavior for this lab.


LCD functions are slow. An ISR should be execute as fast as possible so that other interrupts are not delayed. If LCD is updated inside the ISR, the ISR will be slowed down.
